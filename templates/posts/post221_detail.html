Обзор биржи Gate.io (обзор криптовалютной биржи + комиссии + список монет)

Gate.io — это криптовалютная биржа, базирующаяся на Большом Каймане и запущенная в 2013 году. Gate.io входит в десятку крупнейших криптовалютных бирж в мире по объёму. Сегодня Gate.io обслуживает более 80 миллионов пользователей по всему миру, имеет ежедневный объем торговли более 12 миллиардов долларов и обслуживает пользователей в более чем 224 странах.

Пользователи из США могут использовать биржу, но услуги могут быть более ограниченными, например, доступ к кредитованию, маржинальная торговля, торговля бессрочными контрактами и торговля некоторыми другими монетами.

История Gate.io

Gate.io был запущен в апреле 2013 года, но его первая международная версия была запущена в октябре 2017 года. Его продукт Wallet.io был зарегистрирован в августе 2018 года, но его публичный блокчейн GateChain был создан только в конце 2020 года. В 2021 году количество пользователей Gate.io превысило 8. миллионов, и NFT-бокс выпустил свой первый NFT.

Хотя на момент основания платформа изначально базировалась в Китае, платформа переехала на Каймановы острова из-за усиления регулирования рынка криптовалют в Китае. Gate.io однажды подвергся взлому 7000 BTC в 2015 году, но в 2019 году он был признан второй самой безопасной биржей в мире. 

По состоянию на март 2022 года в компании работает 158 сотрудников, а ее генеральный директор Лин Хан первоначально основал биржу под названием Bter.com. Сегодня Gate.io известна своей ведущей платформой IEO (первичное предложение обмена) и другими передовыми финансовыми услугами. У Gate.io также есть венчурное подразделение и инкубатор стартапов, известный сегодня как Gate.io Labs.

Gate.io лучше всего подходит для:

  • Все типы спотовых инвесторов в криптовалюту и даже маржинальные трейдеры, желающие получить доступ к криптоинструментам и передовым криптофинансовым услугам на бирже высшего уровня, а также к основному предложению нескольких основных криптовалют с большим объемом и ликвидностью. 
  • Трейдеры и инвесторы, которым нужен переменный, основанный на объеме и многоуровневый график комиссий, конкурентоспособный по своим ставкам, а также мощная платформа IEO.

Плюсы и уникальные особенности

Лучшими особенностями Gate.io являются обширный набор продуктов: спотовая торговля, торговля с кредитным плечом, ETF с кредитным плечом, торговля фьючерсами, количественная торговля, обеспеченные кредиты под криптоактивы, а также возможность зарабатывать проценты в криптовалюте.

Помимо этого набора продуктов, Gate.io также предлагает обширную экосистему услуг, связанных с криптовалютой, включая публичный блокчейн под названием GateChain . Эти экосистемы включают GateToken, Gate.io Ventures , Block.info, HipoDeFi, Wallet.io, NFT Magic Box с набором предложений NFT и сопутствующих услуг, а также Gate Labs. 

Что касается криптовалютных предложений , биржа предлагает более 1300 монет и 2500 пар, что является самым обширным предложением на мировом рынке. Пользователи могут покупать криптовалюту с помощью кредитной карты или банковским переводом. Gate.io также предлагает пользователям средство отслеживания данных с агрегированными данными о ликвидациях фьючерсов, позициях по контрактам и т. д.

Помимо спотовой торговли, биржа также предлагает одноранговые push-транзакции и флэш-свопы для обмена одной валюты на другую в один клик. Что касается криптофинансовых услуг, портал добычи ликвидности — это место, где пользователи могут получать доход от комиссий, предоставляя ликвидность. Также предусмотрены условия для парачейн-аукционов и слот-аукционов, а также для облачного майнинга. 

Новая функция для трейдеров называется Copy Trading , которая позволяет трейдерам копировать стратегии. Что касается самого интерфейса обмена и торговли, расширенные графики интегрированы с TradingView, а книги ордеров и расширенные типы ордеров доступны для использования более продвинутыми трейдерами. Пользователи могут выбирать как стандартный, так и профессиональный торговый интерфейс, а также мобильные приложения для Apple и Android, с помощью которых пользователи могут размещать заказы и обмениваться активами.

Gate.io предлагает еще несколько нишевых услуг, таких как раздел стартапов/ скидок и многое другое. Gate.io также предлагает институциональные услуги для учреждений с VIP-привилегиями и другие индивидуальные решения, помимо тех, которые предлагаются только розничным клиентам. 

У биржи есть собственный токен GateToken (GT), который дает трейдерам небольшие преимущества в торговых комиссиях. Это собственный токен основной сети Gatechain. 

Кроме того, существует внебиржевой отдел для покупки и продажи больших объемов криптовалюты без проскальзывания. Okcoin предлагает высокую ликвидность и быстрое исполнение сделок с помощью этой услуги.

Наконец, Gate.io также запускает программу грантов для поддержки разработчиков, работающих на GateChain и за его пределами. Что касается поддержки клиентов , Gate.io предлагает поддержку по электронной почте и вход в систему для получения заявок на помощь. Команда также ведет блог с анонсами. 

Cons & Disadvantages

The main disadvantages of Gate.io are its sometimes poor English wording on its webpages and its relative lack of regulatory clarity for US users. 

Additionally, many complain that the exchange is not very user friendly. Its services and offerings are increasingly varied and advanced and given its large crypto market offerings, they are best suited for more advanced crypto users.

Lastly, the deposit fees are on the higher end as are the trading fees for users at the lowest volume tier (flat 0.20% for both makers and takers) without accounting for any reductions for holders of its native GT exchange token. The exchange also does not allow fiat withdrawals. Users would have to withdraw crypto assets to another exchange that has and allows fiat off-ramps to cash their crypto out for fiat.

Some users maintain that the exchange is sending unclear signals as to whether US users can use the exchange or not as its terms of service and official status in the US are unclear, while the signup page for new users says US users are not supported. The help section says US users are allowed to use the exchange, and US users are not mentioned as banned in the TOS. Some webpages are in Chinese but most are in English.

Lastly, the exchange does require various levels of KYC requirements to be completed to use the exchange for all jurisdictions as per its KYC/AML policy, so this may be a con for those looking for a KYC-free service or exchange.

Gate.io Fees

Gate.io uses a variable, maker-taker fee model with tiers based on both transaction volume and maker status.

Trading fees are incurred when an order is filled by the exchange’s matching engine. If an order does not execute, there is no fee charged until it does execute at a later point, or it can be canceled. The full fee schedule can be found here. 

Taker fees are charged on market orders that are filled immediately (takers of liquidity from the market), and maker fees are charged on orders that are not filled immediately but rather are placed in the orderbook as limit orders (adding to the market liquidity). 

Market orders are always charged taker fees since they take volume from the orderbook, while limit orders are always charged maker fees since they add liquidity and volume to the orderbook. If an order goes on the order book partially or fully (a maker order), any subsequent trades coming from that order will be as a “maker.”

The standard spot trading fee rate is at 0.2% for both makers and takers, while paying fees with Gate.io Points grants users a 0.06% maker/taker fee rate. When paying fees with GT, the maker fee is 0.2% and the taker fee is 0.15% for the standard fee tier.

Account Tiers & Limits

All users of Gate.io are required to undergo KYC verification procedures as per its KYC/AML terms. 

Users who perform successful Level 2 KYC (“Advanced Identity Verification”) can enjoy increased transaction limits. All users have to complete at the minimum Level 1 KYC to obtain access to funding the exchange wallet. 

Users without any levels of KYC cannot withdraw funds, use credit cards or debit cards to buy crypto, and cannot participate in Startup projects at all.

The requirements for documents for KYC and account tiers can be noted here. 

For KYC overall, users are required to submit their name, address, DOB, nationality, government-issued ID, and a valid photo holding said ID, in addition to a valid email address and a mobile phone number.

As far as other limits, there are minimum and maximum withdrawal limits for single transactions which can be checked on the withdrawal page of each coin the user wishes to withdraw. 

The daily limit can be raised while the maximum withdrawal limit is not to be raised. Users can submit a ticket to raise their daily limit. The maximum withdrawal limit is otherwise set at 100 BTC per day.

Users should be very careful not to store crypto long term inside of a Gate.io account as per its official terms, the exchange may seize crypto assets left untouched for two years. Additionally, it may impose KYC restrictions if a user deposits funds into the account before verifying KYC, which is allowed. 

Crypto Security

Gate.io takes several steps to ensure optimal exchange security. Firstly, the exchange uses its own self-developed trading system that has been audited by professional auditing companies to pass their security tests. All website data is transmitted via an encrypted TLS connection for secure data transfer as well. There is a firewall present to deal with illegal intrusions and any data tampering. 

Additionally, there exists malware defense systems and anti-DDOS attack systems as well to achieve higher security. All internal team computers are equipped with endpoint protection mechanisms that are protected by corporate firewalls. 

Accounts also use multi-factor authentication so users are prompted for deposit, withdrawal, and trade orders. Users also have access to securing their accounts via SMS, Google OTP, email address, Yubikeys, IP address monitoring, withdrawal whitelists, funding passwords, anti-phishing codes, and more such as login password.

The Gate.io cold wallet is separated from its hot wallet solution and most assets are stored in the cold wallet. The exchange also adopts an institutional level multi-signature wallet. After an account’s security settings are changed, the user cannot withdraw for 24 hours minimum. Gate proposes a scheme using Merkle tree + third-party auditing. Users can verify that 100% of their assets are indeed stored on Gate.io. GATE also hired an independent virtual currency accounting and auditing company to help with the audit. Gate.io also maintains a bug bounty on HackenProof. 

More security information from the Gate.io team can be found here.

Gate.io Review Conclusion

Gate.io is a good choice of a top tier exchange for all sorts of spot and margin traders around the world who desire access to its advanced suite of crypto financial services products along with extensive selection of crypto trading pairs. 

While the exchange has been hacked a handful of times, its security procedures are adequate now and in line with those of other top tier exchanges. However, the exchange is unregulated and has a confusing TOS that makes it unclear whether or not US users are allowed to use the exchange or not. KYC is additionally required for withdrawals, which makes using Gate.io a risky proposition for users in jurisdictions that are unclear whether or not they can use the exchange.

Otherwise, the exchange offers a wide range of lending, financial, and institutional services and even discounts for users of its native exchange token. Users can also enjoy market maker rebates as well as high volume trading incentives with lower fees.

  • Gate.io offers access to 1300+ cryptos and simple to advanced trading interfaces, mobile apps, futures trading, leveraged ETFs, quantitative trading, secured loans on crypto assets, interest products, and even its own public blockchain network called GateChain
  • Intermediate to advanced traders trading margin or futures who desire access to even more advanced trading instruments without KYC or with more clear regulatory outlook, would prefer to pick a different exchange such as Binance or FTX

Other Alternatives

For customers who desire access to a simpler user interface and less complicated products,, or those who do not desire to participate in cryptocurrency futures trading either Bittrex, OKX, Coinbase, and Voyager can make great alternatives, however with a much smaller amount of cryptocurrencies offered to trade.

Beginners, buy-and-hold investors, and low volume traders may prefer the easy to use features and functionality of Coinbase with its brand presence, US regulatory approval, and cryptocurrency education that even provides the chance to earn free cryptocurrency for learning. This way, US users can be assured that they can use the exchange without any regulatory issues.

Coinbase is best designed for buy-and-hold investors or casual to intermediate cryptocurrency users though has a much smaller selection of cryptocurrencies offered compared to Gate.io—402 pairs vs. Gate.io’s 1300+ coins—while active traders who need access to order books and advanced charting functionality may prefer using Coinbase Pro, FTX, Binance, or KuCoin though their fee structures and product offerings differ, and they all use volume-tiered fee schedules just like Gate.io.

For customers who desire to trade and invest in stocks in addition to top cryptocurrencies and do not mind having a smaller selection from which to choose or only desire access to the top 20 cryptos on the market, Webull and Robinhood are brokers that offers US equities and a small selection of cryptocurrencies. 

Other competitors to Gate.io include OKX, OKcoin, and Kraken.

Gate.io vs Coinbase 

Coinbase is better in some aspects of functionality and regulatory clarity but does not offer as many trading product options when compared to Gate.io.

Gate.io offers margin trading, but Coinbase does not. Also, Gate.io wins in the fee department, as the base volume tier starts at 0.20% which is a bit lower than at Coinbase, and Gate.io’s tiers reduce further with volume, maker status, and even give rebates, especially for holders of its native token. 

Coinbase is a public US company and offers transparency into its industry-leading security along with its brand reputation, while Gate.io is not registered in the US and it is not clear whether or not US users can confidently use Gate.io without getting barred from their account and assets seized. All US users should practice caution and stick to alternatives such as Coinbase.

Ultimately, advanced users who desire both competitive fees and a greater selection of trading products than what Coinbase offers may find the choices below equally valuable.

Gate.io vs FTX 

FTX will win against Gate.io for advanced traders, unless specifically seeking Gate.io’s IEO products or suite of services. FTX offers 323 coins and 492 trading pairs, which is far lower than those of Gate.io, albeit plenty if compared to the global crypto market options.

Derivatives traders who want to trade margin, extensive crypto futures pairs, or leveraged tokens and options will prefer FTX since Gate.io does offer derivatives and margin however the infrastructure is not built out or known for this product as it is for FTX.

FTX is known for its extensive futures pairs and top-class matching and liquidation engine for advanced derivatives traders, while Gate.io also offers a full-featured trading experience with high trading volume, however FTX is known for catering to active derivatives traders and optimizing for high order volume and trading execution speed.

FTX cannot be used by US persons and neither can Gate.io can, though FTX offers a US version of its exchange whose trade offerings are far more limited than its parent global exchange and do not at all compare to FTX International’s selection, but still wins as a choice compared to Gate.io.

International users who can use FTX International may prefer FTX for these reasons over Gate.io. US users trading at FTX US need to do KYC procedures and likewise for Gate.io, KYC is required but it is unclear for US users if they can use the exchange or not. Fees are slightly more competitive at FTX, since FTX offers both fee incentives for volume and for holders of its FTT token.

Gate.io vs Gemini

Gate.io and Gemini are two exchanges with some similarities in their offerings, however Gemini takes the more direct and simple route with its offerings and has the advantage of regulatory clarity, while Gate.io’s offerings are much more complicated to use.

There is a slight difference in fees to start, giving Gate.io the edge for solely fees: the minimum fee tier at Gemini starts at 0.35% for takers and 0.10% for makers, with a flat transaction fee between $0.99-$2.99 and 1.49% transaction fee for trades over $200, so there are extra fees per transaction and extra “auction” fees, while Gate.io starts at 0.20% for those not holding its exchange GT token and also offers volume-tiered fees and even maker rebates.

US investors and traders are allowed to use Gemini with KYC compliance since Gemini is regulated and based in the USA, however as a result, Gemini offers no margin or futures products, while Gate.io is not officially clear for its usage for US users, and is not regulated either, but does offer margin.

Gemini offers 62 coins and 86 trading pairs which is much smaller as compared to that of Gate.io’s 1300+ coins selection. Gate.io also offers far more advanced crypto financial services and products than Gemini does.

Gate.io vs Kraken

Kraken offers margin trading at up to 5X leverage even for US investors and several other margin offerings, which is a win over Gate.io’s lack of regulatory clarity or clear offerings to US users. Simply put, US users may prefer Kraken for its regulatory compliance and strong track record.

Kraken uses a maker-taker fee schedule, starting at 0.16% maker fees and 0.26% taker fees at the entry level which is a bit more competitive even at the entry volume tier level than Gate.io’s 0.20% maker/taker entry level volume tiered fee structure. 

Kraken offers a large overall variety of cryptocurrencies and pairs (over 90 coins, over 400 pairs), but this is not quite as large as that of Gate.io (1300+ coins), so users who value a large selection and advanced traders who seek margin will prefer Kraken by far.

If an active trader wishes to trade other instruments beyond crypto and prefers not to KYC, he or she may prefer other exchanges such as Bybit or OKX, but if he or she prefers a much larger selection of crypto only instruments and does not mind KYC, Kraken wins. 

Kraken is accessible in 48 US states with KYC and is regulated and licensed by FinCEN in the USA, while US users cannot clearly use Gate.io and it is not regulated.

Gate.io vs Binance

Binance is the leading global crypto established exchange by trading volume, offering a similar amount of coins as Gate.io—over 351 coins and over 1300 pairs.

Traders looking solely for the most advanced trading options such as high leverage margin and futures products specific to crypto only will prefer Binance, as it offers many USDT futures pairs that are not offered even on FTX, the other derivatives leader, at the moment. 

Binance’s maker-taker fee schedule is better in its competitiveness compared to that of Gate.io, starting at 0.1%, however Binance also offers further 25% reduction in fees if paid in BNB, so this is a win for Binance, alongside its offering of reduced fee tiers for higher volume and even maker rebates. 

Binance also offers a more extensive web and ecosystem of products, support, and liquidity, especially given its larger daily volume as compared to Gate.io, though Gate.io is still in the top 10 exchanges for daily volume. Binance is the most popular and well-known futures market in the global cryptocurrency industry and well-known for its extensive futures pairs. 

The Binance exchange is not at all accessible to US persons and US customers will have to use Binance US which is a much more limited version of its global parent site, with only 64 coins and 130 pairs, which is a lot more limited than either Binance International or Gate.io both, however it is well regulated for US users. The main difference therefore between Binance and Gate.io is regarding their scope of advanced financial services and their offerings such as launchpads as well as their regulatory outlook and official terms of use for US investors. 

Binance requires full KYC now to trade even spot products, and Gate.io requires full KYC compliance as well to use, but unlike for Binance or Binance US, it is not clear how US users can use Gate.io.

Advanced traders and intermediate users alike who value a large selection of cryptocurrencies, products like staking, lending, and more, competitive fee structures, and futures and margin products will probably prefer Binance, but Gate.io may win in regards for its unique IEO offerings for which it is known.
